Who We Are?

At Energy Technical Associates, we deliver innovative elevator and escalator solutions with a focus on safety, eco-friendliness, and exceptional service. Based in Abu Dhabi, UAE, we’ve partnered with industry leaders like Mitsubishi, Hyundai, and Otis to redefine vertical mobility.

Our Mission

To revolutionize urban mobility through cutting-edge technology, ensuring safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction while adhering to global standards like EN-81 and EN-6.
